Villagers’ AI

Your villagers act autonomously based on their needs (hunger, fatigue, socializing). They’ll work, rest, and socialize without constant micromanagement, letting you focus on strategic decisions.

Dinosaurs’ AI

Dinosaurs can be tamed or wild. Tamed dinos help defend, carry goods, or even race—adding a whole new layer of strategy and fun to your tribal experience.

Project Progress - From October to May

Dive into our monthly milestones, from ideation to final defense.

Detailed Timeline

October - Project Kickoff

Start
Game Mechanics begun. Visual Assets begun. Website started in parallel.

Initial brainstorming, setting up version control, and dividing tasks among the team.

December - Methodology Defense

Game Mechanics at ~20% AI Dev at 0% Assets ~30% Multiplayer 0% Sound Design ~30% Resource Balancing ~10% UI/UX ~20% Website ~70%

Presentation of methodology to ensure the project is on track. The website is almost done already!

January - 1st Tech Defense

Mechanics ~40% AI Dev ~20%, officially started now Assets ~60% Multiplayer 0% Sound ~50% Balancing ~30% UI/UX ~40% Website: 100% Complete

First technical demo showcasing partial AI, basic mechanics, and nearly finished site.

March - 2nd Tech Defense

Mechanics ~80% AI Dev ~70% Assets 100% Multiplayer ~60% Sound ~90% Balancing ~70% UI/UX ~80%

Second defense focusing on robust AI behaviors, nearly final mechanics and visuals.

May - Final Defense

100%
All tasks completed: Mechanics, AI, Assets, Multiplayer, Sound, Balancing, UI/UX, Website.

Final presentation and release. Players can now fully experience the prehistoric world of Tribz!
